SMB Market Opportunity & Audience Segmentation

Primary SMB Audience: Independent OB/GYN Practices

  • Market Characteristics: 1,800+ independent practices, 3-8 physicians each, typical revenue $2-8M annually
  • SMB Pain Points: Budget constraints, limited IT resources, need for cost-effective efficiency tools
  • Opportunity: 70% lack standardized mental health screening protocols, creating clear value proposition
  • Decision Makers: Practice managers and lead physicians focused on ROI and workflow integration
Data Sources: AMA Practice Census 2024, Healthcare Financial Management Association (HFMA) SMB Healthcare Survey

SMB-Focused Value Proposition

  • 40% Cost Reduction: Directly addresses SMB budget sensitivity through automation
  • 25% Detection Improvement: Enhances practice reputation and patient outcomes
  • Seamless Integration: HIPAA-compliant EHR connectivity minimizes implementation friction
  • Scalable Pricing: Tiered model accommodates practices from 5-50 providers
