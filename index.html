Key Insight: SMB Sales Cycles Are Different

Unlike enterprise sales, small practices make decisions quickly but need lots of reassurance. They can't afford mistakes, so every touchpoint had to build trust. We focused on peer recommendations, transparent pricing, and risk-free trials rather than feature demos.
